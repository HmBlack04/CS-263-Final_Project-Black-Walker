Ethical Lenses

In order to make decisions about how these algorithms should be used, it can be helpful to examine the situation through different ethical lenses. Here are a few popular ones, as described by Santa Clara University:

Rights - What action best protects the moral rights of everybody involved?

Justice - What action ensures justice for everybody involved?

Utilitarian - What action creates the greatest ratio of good vs. harm for society as a whole?

Common Good - What action is best for every individual in the community?

Virtue - What action best reflects the type of person you want to be personally?

Care Ethics - What action reflects care for each individual on a more holistic level?
